Yield:
6 Servings

Per Serving:
1 Lean protein
3 Condiments
Just need to add some greens to this one!  

## Ingredients
- [ ] 3 cups of freshly shredded low-fat cheddar cheese

### For the beef taco filling
- [ ] 1 1/4 pound ground beef
- [ ] 1/2 cup onion, diced
- [ ] 3 garlic cloves, chopped
- [ ] 1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
- [ ] 3 tsp taco seasoning
- [ ] 1/3 cup of water (if needed)

## Directions
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F.
2. Place cheese in piles on a non-greased baking sheet, bake for 8 minutes until piles have melted into circles.
3. Don't let the cheese color too much. let cool 2 minutes on the baking sheet. Put on paper towels blot off excess grease.
4. To form shells, lay over a propped-up utensil. (Or you can use- a wide bowl or saucepan) Set aside to cool.

Taco filling:
Make taco filling according to the package. Then add onion, garlic, and jalapenos.
