Yield:
3 Servings

Per serving:
1 Lean
3 Vegetables
1 Condiment

## Ingredients
- [ ] 18 ounces Jennie-O hot Italian turkey sausage
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups zucchini into noodles
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups peppers
- [ ] 1 1/2 cup no salt added petite diced tomatoes
- [ ] 1 1/2 tsp. Italian dried herbs
- [ ] 1/2 tsp garlic salt

## Directions
1. Cook sausage in a large frying pan
2. Once cooked cut into bite-size pieces and return to pan
3. Add tomatoes, vegetables, and spices. Cook for 3-5 minutes or until vegetables are at the desired tenderness
