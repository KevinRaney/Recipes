Makes 3 servings
Per serving
1 Lean
3 vegetables
1 1/3 condiment

## Ingredients
- [ ] 18 ounces Jennie•O hot Italian turkey sausage (3 leaner)
- [ ] 1 ½ Cups (6 oz) zucchini into noodles (3 greens)
- [ ] 1 ½ Cups (7.89 oz) Peppers (3 greens)
- [ ] 1 ½ cup (9.51 oz) No salt added Petite Diced tomatoes (3 greens)
- [ ] 1 ½ tsp. Italian dried herbs (3 condiments)
- [ ] ½ tsp garlic powder (1 condiment)

## Directions
1. Put the sausage on a large frying pan.
2. Once cooked cut into bite-size pieces and return to pan.
3. Add tomatoes, vegetables, and spices.
4. Cook for 3-5 min or until vegetables are desired tenderness.
