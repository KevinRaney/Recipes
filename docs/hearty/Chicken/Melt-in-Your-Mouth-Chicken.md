Yield:
4 servings

Per serving:
1 Leaner Lean
2.5 Condiments
(You still need 1 Healthy Fat per serving)

## Ingredients
- [ ] 28 oz raw boneless, skinless, chicken breasts or tenderloins - should cook down to 18 oz
- [ ] 1- 1/2 cups low fat plain Greek yogurt - I used Fage 2% plain Greek yogurt
- [ ] 1 tsp garlic powder
- [ ] 3/4 tsp salt
- [ ] 1/2 tsp pepper
- [ ] 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Line a 13 x 9-inch baking pan with non-stick foil. This is optional but it makes clean up easier.
3. Place chicken in a baking pan in a single layer.
4. Combine yogurt with Parmesan cheese and seasonings in a medium-sized bowl.
5. Distribute yogurt mixture evenly over chicken.
6. Bake 45 minutes or until done. Be careful not to overcook.
