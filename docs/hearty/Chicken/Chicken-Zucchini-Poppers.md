1 leaner
3 green
3 condiments

## Ingredients
- [ ] 1 lb. ground chicken breast
- [ ] 2 cups grated zucchini (leave the peel on)
- [ ] 2-3 green onions, sliced
- [ ] 3-4 Tbsp cilantro, minced
- [ ] 1 clove garlic, minced
- [ ] ¾ tsp salt
- [ ] ½ tsp pepper

## Directions
1. Mix ground chicken and remaining ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Grease frying pan or skillet with olive oil.
2. Spoon out 8-10 nugget-sized pieces onto the oiled skillet (I like to use a small ice-cream scoop). Cook over medium heat for 5 minutes on each side.
3. Serve plain or with favorite approved dipping sauce.
