Yield:

1 Serving

Per serving:
1 Lean
3 Green
2 Condiments
1 Healthy Fat

## Ingredients
- [ ] 6 oz chicken (8 oz before cooked) sliced open and stuffed with one wedge of laughing cow lite cheese
- [ ] 2 servings of broccoli
- [ ] 1 serving of cauliflower
- [ ] Sprinkle with 1 oz shredded cheese

## Directions

1. Sprinkled with
2. 1 shake of salt and pepper
3. 1 sprinkle of dry ranch mix
4. Bake at 360 for 30 minutes or until cooked through
