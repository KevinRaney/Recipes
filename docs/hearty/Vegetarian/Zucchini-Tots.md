# Zucchini Tots!! 🥒

## Ingredients
- [ ] 2 cups grated zucchini
- [ ] 1/2 cup sliced green onions
- [ ] 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 1/2 cup cheese (low fat)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Fresh garlic
- [ ] ¼ tsp Salt
- [ ] ¼ tsp pepper

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
2. Grate the zucchini into a clean dish towel until you have 2 packed cups. Wring all of the excess water out of the zucchini, there will be a lot of water. In a medium bowl, combine all of the ingredients and season with salt and pepper.
3. Spoon 1 tablespoon of mixture in your hands and roll into small ovals. Place on the cookie sheet and bake at 400 for 16 to 18 minutes, turning halfway though cooking until golden.

Makes 4 servings
Per serving
¾ ounce protein
1 ¼ vegetable serving
2 1/2 condiments
